    Standing Notes — Tickets, Crews & Tactics

    Lodging

    Cabana Bay Beach Resort, 2 rooms booked Jun 14–20.

    Each room carries a $300 dining credit — $600 total, hotel-only, and it must be used by Friday night.

    Tickets & Express

    All 6 over age 3 hold a 5-Day Park-to-Park ticket, Volcano Bay included.

    A 2-Day Express on Thu + Fri covers everyone except Grandma. Express never works at Volcano Bay; at Epic it is single-use per ride.

    Add-Ons

    2 interactive wands — shared, bought Monday at Ollivanders / Hogsmeade, working all week.

    3 Power-Up Bands for Jace, Avia & Oz, activated Thursday at Super Nintendo World.

    The Crew Model

    Family Crew = Grandma + a parent + Oz (43″) + Elowen, plus Jace & Avia when not coastering.

    Coaster Crew = a parent + Avia when she wants in. Jace skips coasters.

    Dining Tactic

    Hotel breakfasts & dinners on the credit; park lunches out of pocket via mobile order ~20 min ahead (one adult orders and grabs a table).

    Exceptions: Toadstool Cafe (Epic) = virtual waitlist at park open; Minion Cafe (USF) = table delivery.

    Arrivals & Departure

    Sun Jun 14 AM — Grandma, Becca & Elowen fly in, check in both rooms.

    Mon Jun 15, 4:30 AM — Zach, Jace, Avia & Oz land MCO (red-eye). SuperStar Shuttle; no rental car — Universal buses & walking.

    Sat Jun 20 — 9 AM flight, ~6 AM hotel departure, no park.
